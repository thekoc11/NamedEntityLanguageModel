
     Recipes with the Category string "Londontowne", the bulk of the file
LONDONTN, are from a collection of favorite recipes of members of the London
Town Publik House Assembly and their friends. Donations and proceeds from sales
of the recipes in printed form are used to obtain authentic furnishings for the
London Town Publik House, a restored 18th Century Inn and National Historic
Landmark.
